Credits

While atrogue has been developed from scratch, many conceptual ideas, creatures and object kinds have been adapted from old Rogue-like games; namely from a Linux port by Alan Cox in the early 1990s of a Rogue clone originally developed by Tim Stoehr ("rogue 5.3-clone").

Some of the more modern ideas have been adapted from NetHack.
